Hi! My name is Chris, I'm a post-doctoral researcher working with the Louisiana State University rice breeding program at the intersection of quantitative genetics, data science, and applied plant breeding.

Current research

Download a pdf version my CV

I am post-doctoral researcher working in the lab of Dr. Adam Famoso at Louisiana State University. The Famoso lab is part of the LSU AgCenter and runs one of the biggest public rice breeding programs in the Southern US. I am based at Cornell University as a visiting scholar in the Robbins Lab where I also work in close collaboration with the McCouch lab. The aim of my research is to determine the best strategy for integrating new breeding technologies into the LSU progam.

Past research

I completed my PhD in 2019 at Cornell University in the Section of Plant Breeding and Genetics in the School of Integrated Plant Sciences. My PhD advisor was Dr. Michael Mazourek, and my research project was focused on understanding and improving fruit quality traits in winter squash.
